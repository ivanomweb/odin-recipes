Pasta Carbonara

Description

The pasta Carbonara is perhaps one fo the most famous dishes of the italian cuisine. It's tasty, creamy and... yummy! You should really try it!

Ingredients (for 2 people)

  • 200g of mezze maniche;
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g of guanciale (the one the comes in small cubes)
  • Grated Parmiggiano cheese.
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Preparation

As you can see this recipe needs just a few (good) ingredients

  1. First pour water in a pot and let it get to the boiling point.
  2. Add the pasta to it and let it cook from 9 to 11 minutes.
  3. Add a little less than a spoon of salt.
  4. Meanwhile let's prepare the sauce. Take a bowl.
  5. Add 1 full egg and the whites of the two other eggs.
  6. Add a tsp of black pepper and parmiggiano cheese.
  7. Mix them with a fork until you get a smooth texture.
  8. Meanwhile take a small frying pan and throw in your guanciale cubes at low flame.
  9. Once the pasta is ready drain the water with the help of a colander.
  10. Pute the pasta in the frying pan with the guanciale and then turn of the flame.
  11. When you've done this try pour the sauce in the frying pan and mix everything together.
  12. Your Carbonara is ready!
