Sorbetto al limone

Description

A fresh, juicy and tasty dessert to drink in a hot summer.

Ingredients for 600g

  • 130g of lemon juice;
  • 170g of sugar;
  • 30g of egg yolk;
  • 30g of Limoncello;
  • 250g of water.

Preparation

  1. Pute the yolk in a recepient and start mixing it.
  2. Add 30g of sugar and keep on mixing.
  3. Squeeze the lemons to make the juice.
  4. Pour the lemon juice in a recepient with water.
  5. Add the remaining 140g of sugar and add the yolks.
  6. Add Limoncello.
  7. Put everything in the ice cream maker for 40 mins and it will be ready to serve!
